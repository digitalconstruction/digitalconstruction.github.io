Digital Construction Ontologies

A suite of ontologies for digital construction:

  • Entities: https://w3id.org/digitalconstruction/Entities - Identifiable entities in construction, including their classifications, breakdowns and groupings

  • Processes: https://w3id.org/digitalconstruction/Processes - Activities, capabilities, constraints and variables

  • Agents: https://w3id.org/digitalconstruction/Agents - The actors and stakeholders, including persons and organizations and their relations (including contracts)

  • Information: https://w3id.org/digitalconstruction/organization - Information content entities, such as designs, plans, events, and issues

  • Contexts: https://w3id.org/digitalconstruction/organization - Contexts for data (planned/actual, as-designed/as-built, levels of detail)

Constributing projects:

  • Diction - Development of shared situational awareness in construction projects

  • BIM4EEB - BIM-based toolkit for efficient renovation of residential buildings
