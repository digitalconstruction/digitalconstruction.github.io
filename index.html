Digital Construction Ontologies

Digital technologies are increasingly used in all stages of construction lifecycle. Existing buildings and sites are mapped with scanning technologies, designs are created using the tools of building information modeling (BIM), the design and management information is consumed at the site through mobile devices, the progress of construction is monitored with positioning, occupancy and activity sensors as well as with scanning technologies, facility management is based on data gathered by building automation systems (BAS), sensors, and so on.

Digital construction means that large volumes of data will be produced at each stage of construction lifecycle. It is a significant challenge to process and interrelate different pieces of data into a meaningful and accurate overall picture that is operationally useful and understandable for both human and automated agents.

Digital construction ontologies attempt to address the semantic level of this challenge, by developing a suite of ontologies that capture essential concepts and properties faced in construction and renovation projects.

Ontology suite

Entities https://w3id.org/digitalconstruction/Entities Identifiable entities in construction, including their classifications, breakdowns and groupings
Processes https://w3id.org/digitalconstruction/Processes Activities, capabilities, constraints and variables
Agents https://w3id.org/digitalconstruction/Agents The actors and stakeholders, including persons and organizations and their relations and contracts
Information https://w3id.org/digitalconstruction/Information Information content entities, such as designs, plans, events, and issues
Contexts https://w3id.org/digitalconstruction/Contexts Multi-contexts data (planned/actual, as-designed/as-built, levels of detail)
OccupantBehavior https://w3id.org/digitalconstruction/OccupantBehavior Occupant behavior and comfort
IndoorAirQuality https://w3id.org/digitalconstruction/IndoorAirQuality Indoor air quality
BuildingAcoustics https://w3id.org/digitalconstruction/BuildingAcoustics Building acoustics
EnergySystems https://w3id.org/digitalconstruction/EnergySystems Energy systems of buildings
